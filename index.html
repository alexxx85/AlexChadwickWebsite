FAQ

How much do you usually charge?

I usually charge around $20 for a logo, and for a website it highly depends on the type of website I'm developing. If it's one page it will cost around $100

How much time will it require for you to finish a project?

This depends on the complexity of the project. I am able to do websites in a day, or sometimes in a week. Price also plays an important role in time, since I will prioritize better paying projects!

What methods of payment are available at the moment?

At the moment I only accept PayPal

How are payments done?

Payments are simple to do. After we discuss details and I decide if I want to take the comission, and we decide a price I will give you my PayPal email and you will have to pay 50% of the final price. After I finish you will send the other 50% and I will send you the project.
